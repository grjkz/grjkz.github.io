About Me

My name is Philip Choi and I was born and raised in NYC. I graduated from college and went to work in Japan for two years before returning to America once again, to seek a career.

Growing up in the early stages of developing technology, it was natural for me to have an interest. I didn't have the luxury of getting my hands on what I wanted so through friends and school is where most of my exposure took place. I'm always curious about how things work and enjoy disassembling them to figure it out, be it hardware or the mind.

Professional Goal

I enjoy learning and being great at what I do. I look forward to learning more about web development programming look forward to ultimately entering the gaming industry, creating great products that will enamor others.

Technical Skills

JavaScript, Ruby on Rails, HTML5, CSS3, jQuery, Node.js, Backbone, PostgreSQL, SQLite, Foundation, GIT, C++, Socket.io, Phaser, Sinatra

Interests

My hobbies mostly revolve around video games. My favorite genres are RPG and action-adventure. I am also great at first person shooters but I prefer to play them on the PC. My other hobbies include eating, bowling, billiards, snowboarding, handball, biking/cycling, and kyudo (Japanese archery).
