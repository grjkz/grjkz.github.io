About Me

My name is Philip Choi and I was born and raised in NYC. After graduating from college, I went to work in Japan for two years before returning to America once again, to seek a career.

Growing up in the early stages of developing technology, it was natural for me to have an interest. I didn't have the luxury of getting my hands on what I wanted so through friends and school is where most of my exposure took place. I'm always curious about how things work and enjoy disassembling them to figure it out, be it hardware or the mind.

Professional Goal

I enjoy learning and being great at what I do. I look forward to learning more about web development programming look forward to ultimately entering the gaming industry, creating great products that will enamor others.

Fresco News

Check it out!

A landing page I created for Fresco News. It includes a nested carousel for images and articles. Supports IE8+ history API, enabling backward and forward SPA browser navigation for those with trigger-happy fingers. Velocity.js is used for many of the animations and media querying. It is also responsive for small devices.

Try scrolling after opening the "Tryout Fresco News" card in "News Dashboard"

  • Slick
  • Velocity
  • html5-history-api
  • jQuery
